How RTU-X works

RTU-X operates as an upstream optimization layer designed to reduce waste while preserving OEM safety behavior.

🧠

Upstream control intercept

RTU-X intercepts standard 24V control calls and applies timing logic intended to reduce inefficient cycling and unnecessary runtime.

🛡

Fail-safe OEM behavior

The system is designed so loss of power returns the unit to standard OEM pass-through behavior.

📈

Measurement support

CT-based monitoring and runtime logging support a disciplined savings verification process during pilot evaluation.

Warranty-conscious, non-invasive architecture

RTU-X is designed to stay upstream, preserve OEM safeties, and avoid common modification risks.

Designed to preserve OEM behavior
Optimization upstream. Factory safeties remain intact.
✅
  • No refrigerant system modification
  • No OEM control board replacement
  • No bypass of factory safeties
  • Fail-safe OEM pass-through on loss of power
Important boundaries
Common actions that often create unnecessary risk.
⚠️
  • No tapping into sealed refrigerant lines
  • No disabling safeties or limit switches
  • No compressor shell modifications
  • No operating outside OEM rated parameters
Warranty policies vary by OEM; RTU-X is designed to operate within OEM-oriented boundaries.
